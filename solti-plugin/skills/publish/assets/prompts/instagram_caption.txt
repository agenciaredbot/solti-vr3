## Instagram Best Practices
1. First line is the hook — make it scroll-stopping
2. 300-800 characters ideal for caption
3. Emojis OK but tasteful (2-4 max)
4. Up to 15 hashtags (put in first comment or at end)
5. Include a CTA ("guarda este post", "comenta si...", "comparte con...")
6. Write in Spanish matching my-voice.md tone

## Structure
- Hook: Emotional or curiosity-driven opener
- Body: 2-3 sentences of value
- CTA: Clear action to take
- Hashtags: 10-15 relevant ones
